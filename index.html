⚡ Match-up · Players to Watch

Match context: Egypt drew 1–1 with Belgium in MD1 — they took the lead, but a Mohamed Hany own goal in the 66th minute cost them the win. New Zealand drew 2–2 with Iran — Chris Wood delivered two assists, showing exactly how dangerous he is even without scoring. Egypt must win. A draw keeps New Zealand's knockout hopes alive, a win puts them through. Expect a physical, high-stakes game from kickoff.
